Academics

A Stanford education is not a narrow path that students follow from Point A to Point Z. We encourage our students to explore the academic landscape, venturing into unfamiliar fields of knowledge and, perhaps, discovering new passions that will take them in a different direction altogether. Along the way, faculty members help guide them, and fellow students offer diverse perspectives that can shed new light on the path. We also encourage our students to push the limits of human technology and work to benefit mankind with new breakthrough and technologies. By nurturing this spirit of advancement, Jarvis University aims to prepare global citizens for a life-long love of learning and pushing the boundaries of unnovation across the world.
